Tropical sweetness meets a slow, building heat. Ripe mango lays down the foundation, habanero brings the fire. The balance is the magic — sweet first, then the kick hits just right. Not for the timid.

Jalapeño Blend

The pepper flavor, none of the pain. Seeds and ribs removed so you get all of the jalapeño's earthy, vegetal depth without the heat. Unexpected, refreshing, and surprisingly addictive. A crowd conversation-starter.

Jalapeño Mild Earthy Unique

Island Blend

Close your eyes, take a sip. Creamy coconut and tangy pineapple transport you somewhere with warm sand and no agenda. Tropical, smooth, and unapologetically vacation-in-a-jar.

Mermaid Blend

Where the sea meets the sweetness. Rich coconut swirls with ripe strawberry in a blend that's as dreamy as it looks. Light, lush, and slightly magical — the one that turns the most heads at the table.

Coconut Strawberry Sweet Dreamy

Sea Salted Watermelon

The unexpected combination that makes total sense on the first sip. Juicy, fresh watermelon with a kiss of sea salt that amplifies every note. Sweet, savory, and completely original. You'll think about this one after it's gone.

Watermelon Sea Salt Savory-Sweet Bold

Holiday Blend
